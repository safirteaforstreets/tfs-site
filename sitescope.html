How it works

Capture. Understand. Act.

01 · CAPTURE

A phone becomes a sensor.

A trained operator walks the quarter-mile — no new hardware, no installs. Every condition is photographed and geolocated.

02 · UNDERSTAND

Scored, then confirmed.

Our models flag and size each condition; human QA confirms what matters before anything moves.

03 · ACT

Filed and tracked.

Each condition is filed through the city's channels and followed to closure — with the dates on record.

Trust & privacy

We document conditions, not people.

  • Human in the loop. Every report is reviewed by a person before it's filed.
  • Never for enforcement. Sensitive findings go to outreach and services teams, not enforcement.
  • Scoped to the public realm. We focus on street-level conditions like graffiti, potholes, and dumping.
  • Your data stays yours. Reports are shared with you and the relevant agency — no more.
