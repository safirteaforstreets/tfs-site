Privacy Policy

Your data matters. Understanding our commitment to you.

Last updated: June 19, 2026

Information We Collect

Tea For Streets collects the following categories of information:

  • Street-Level Imagery — photographs and video from public streets and infrastructure areas used for infrastructure analysis and detection.
  • Location & GPS Data — GPS coordinates, street addresses, and geographic metadata associated with imagery and reports. This may include precise location data embedded in image metadata (EXIF).
  • Infrastructure Issue Reports — data about detected or reported infrastructure defects, including category, severity, descriptions, and related attributes.
  • Resident Street Reports — when you report a street or infrastructure issue through our public reporting tool, we collect the issue category and any description, the location (an address, cross-streets, or precise GPS coordinates if you choose “Use my location”), any photo you attach (including location metadata, or EXIF, contained in it), and the email address you provide so we can send you updates.
  • Contact & Lead Information — name, email address, phone number, organization, and inquiry details submitted through contact forms, Microsoft Forms, demo requests, or investor inquiries.
  • Account Information — credentials, profile details, and authentication information if you create or maintain a platform account.
  • Usage & Analytics Data — app or platform usage events, feature interactions, session logs, crash reports, and performance metrics to improve the Service.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Process and analyze street-level imagery using computer vision and machine learning
  • Generate infrastructure intelligence, analytics, dashboards, and reports for authorized users
  • File 311 service requests with the responsible municipal agency (such as LA 311 or SF311) on your behalf and track them through to resolution
  • Send status updates to people who report a street issue (for example, filed, acknowledged, and resolved)
  • Respond to demo requests, investor inquiries, and general contact requests from prospective customers and partners
  • Improve platform performance, reliability, user experience, and model accuracy
  • Monitor and prevent misuse, fraud, or security threats
  • Communicate with users about the Service, updates, and support
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our Terms of Service

Reports & Form Submissions

Demo requests, investor interest submissions, and other contact form inquiries are collected via Microsoft Forms. When you submit a form, your name, email, phone number (if provided), organization, and inquiry details are captured and may be stored by both Tea For Streets and Microsoft. These details are used to respond to your inquiry, schedule demos, provide investor information, or otherwise communicate with you about our platform.

Resident street-issue reports submitted through our public reporting tool are delivered to our team using a third-party form-delivery service and sent to admin@teaforstreets.app. The information in a report — your email, the location and any GPS coordinates, and details of any photo — may then be submitted to the relevant municipal 311 system to request a repair. We use your email only to send you updates about your report.

Data Sharing

Tea For Streets does not sell personal data to third parties for marketing purposes. We may share information only with:

  • Service providers — cloud storage, analytics, security monitoring, email, and platform infrastructure partners
  • Form-delivery providers — report submissions may be processed by a third-party form-delivery service to route them to our team
  • Municipal agencies — when you report a street or infrastructure issue, the report and its location may be submitted to the relevant city’s 311 service to request a repair
  • Legal authorities — when required by law, court order, or government request
  • Business partners — if you are an authorized B2B user, your organization and usage data may be shared with your account administrator

All service providers are contractually required to protect user data and use it only for the purposes described.

Data Retention

We retain personal data and infrastructure intelligence data only as long as necessary to operate the Service, fulfill your requests, or comply with legal obligations. Imagery and derived intelligence may be retained longer to improve model performance and maintain historical records, unless deletion is requested or legally required.

Your Rights & Account Management

If you have created an account or provided personal information through forms, reports, or inquiries, you may request:

  • Access to your personal data
  • Correction of inaccurate information
  • Deletion of your account and associated personal data (subject to legal retention requirements)

To submit any of these requests, email admin@teaforstreets.app. We will process requests within a reasonable timeframe.

Children's Privacy

Tea For Streets is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

Security

Tea For Streets employs industry-standard security measures to protect personal data and imagery from unauthorized access, alteration, and disclosure. However, no security system is impenetrable, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Changes to This Policy

Tea For Streets may update this Privacy Policy to reflect changes in our practices or applicable law. We will notify users of material changes and obtain consent where required. Your continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of updated policies.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, your data, or our privacy practices, contact us at admin@teaforstreets.app.
