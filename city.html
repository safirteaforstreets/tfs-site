How it works

One walk. Measured, filed, tracked.

01 · Survey

We walk the ped shed

Every quarter-mile around a property — the walk a resident actually takes — captured street, curb, and corner.

02 · Measure

To the millimeter

The photo sees it; the LiDAR measures it. Pothole depth and volume, graffiti area, dumping volume — structured, not subjective.

03 · File & track

Through the right channel

Each eligible issue filed as a clean, geolocated 311 request — then tracked until it’s closed.
